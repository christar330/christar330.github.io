Relay

BEERLYMPICS

Start with dizzy bat. On 10 spins, contestants try to run to the "kegs". They must weave in and out of the cones to get to the volleyballs. Once at the volleyballs, they will try to beer pong them into the tubs.

  • Contestants: 2
  • Age: 21+
  • Dash Pack: 6
  • 6 Red Tubs
  • 2 Barrels
  • 6 Cones
  • 2 Plastic Bats
  • 6 volleyballs
  • 2 Beer Goggles

BOUNCY BALL BATTLE JUNIOR

Hippity Hops are lined up at the starting point. Contestants will hop down and around a cone. Sabotage may occur when nearing the cone. Start and end at the same spot.

  • Contestants: 5
  • Age: 7-11
  • Dash Pack: 3
  • 5 Hippity Hops
  • 1 Cone

COSTUME TRIAD

Contestants line up at the starting line. On go, they run to the first cone and do 5 jumping jacks. Then they run to the second cone and do 5 squats. Then they run to the last cone and spin 10 times. Then they run back to the starting spot.

  • Contestants: 3-4
  • Age: 6-9
  • Dash Pack: 2
  • 3-4 blow-up costumes
  • 6 cones

Games

BATTLE BALLS

Contestants are in the bubble soccer inflatables. They will be lined up facing each other with cones on either side of them. The objective is to get through the other person's cones first, like a goal in soccer. Best of 3.

  • Contestants: 2
  • Age: 18+
  • Dash Pack: 3
  • 2 Bubble Soccer Balls
  • 4 Cones

DAD AEROBICS

Contestants are each handed a dance ribbon. When the music starts they will do a dance routine to the music. Fans will then vote by applause who was the best.

  • Contestants: 3-4
  • Age: 30+
  • Dash Pack: 2
  • 3-4 Dance Ribbons
  • 3-4 Blindfolds

DANCING IN THE DARK

Contestants will be blindfolded. Host will tell DJ to start the beat, and the contestants are going to dance. The host will tap on contestants eliminating them, and leave the last person dancing by themselves on the field.

  • Contestants: 4
  • Age: 18+
  • Dash Pack: 2
  • 4 Blindfolds

KEEP YOUR COOL

Teams are placed facing each other. One member from each team is standing next to a bucket of water balloons. The other team member is wearing a colander helmet. The team member next to the bucket will try to throw the water balloons into the colander. Whichever team gets the most balloons in the colander wins.

  • Contestants: 4 (2 teams of 2)
  • Age: 7-11
  • Dash Pack: 2
  • 1 Bucket
  • 10 Water Balloons
  • 2 Colander Helmets

Faceoffs

HIP HOP OR NOT

Trivia based game with host. The contestant will be quizzed on their knowledge about hip hop song lyrics. A lyric will appear on the Dash Board, and the contestant will say "Hip Hop" if they think it is a hip hop lyric, or "Not" if they think it is a country lyric.

  • Contestants: 1
  • Age: 18+
  • Dash Pack: 1
  • 1 Chain
  • 1 Pair of Glasses
  • 1 Hat

COUNTRY OR CROCK

Trivia based game with host. The contestant will be quizzed on their knowledge about country song lyrics. A lyric will appear on the Dash Board, and the contestant will say "Country" if they think it is a country lyric, or "Crock" if they think it is not a country lyric.

  • Contestants: 1
  • Age: 18+
  • Dash Pack: 1
  • 1 Cowboy Hat

FINISH THE LYRIC

game with host. The contestant will listen to a song, and when it stops they must finish the lyric.

  • Contestants: 2
  • Age: 16+
  • Dash Pack: 1
  • No Props

NAME THAT TUNE

Game with host. DJ will play 15 seconds of a tune. The contestant has to try to guess what tune it is.

  • Contestants: 1
  • Age: 18+
  • Dash Pack: 1
  • No Props

Bolt Skits

BOLT'S BARBERSHOP

Bolt will give a "haircut" to a bald man. The intensity will grow with the music.

  • Contestants: 1 bald man
  • Age: middle age
  • Dash Pack: 1
  • 1 Chair
  • Bolt's Barbershop Bucket

BLINDFOLD BASKETBALL

PA will introduce Bolt and contestant as performing a basketball stunt. Contestant will be placed by Bolt and blindfolded. Eventually Bolt will get cold feet and leave the contestant by himself, blindfolded, on the field.

  • Contestants: 1
  • Age: 18-50
  • Dash Pack: 2
  • 1 Blindfold
  • 1 Basketball Hoop
  • 1 Mini Basketball
  • 1 Trampoline

FUN IN THE SUN

Bolt acts as a waiter to the field umpire by having him sit down, serving refreshments, and giving him a sun reflector. Lastly, Bolt brings out a water gun and sprays the umpire. The umpire is not amused and tosses him of the game.

  • Contestants: 1
  • Age: Plant Umpire
  • Dash Pack: 2
  • 1 Sun Reflector
  • 1 Folding Lawn Chair
  • 1 Pair of Sunglasses
  • 1 Fancy Cup
  • 1 Water Gun
  • 1 Towel
